About Us

About Rusangu University

In Rusangu University grounds, you will find the Musangu tree from which the name Rusangu is derived. This historic place was pioneered by the great American missionary and teacher, W. H. Anderson in 1903. The roots of the Adventist work in Zambia are deeply embedded at Rusangu Mission. The vision of Anderson gave birth to Rusangu University which opened its doors in May 2003. Rusangu University has distinguished itself as one of the pioneering private Universities in Zambia registered with the Higher Education Authority (HEA). The programs on offer are accredited by different bodies, local and international.

Our Core Values

These core values reflect our desire for shared beliefs, traits and behavioral norms we shall be expected to display in the discharge of our various roles in pursuit of the University vision and mission. In pursuing our vision and implementing our mission, the core values of the University that will guide our conduct and bind us together are as follows:

Excellence & Christ Centerdness

We have committed ourselves to executing our roles in an excellent manner to the satisfaction of our key stakeholders as well as to reflect Christ’s nature in all our endeavors, which include honesty, authentic spirituality, openness and love.

Respect & Integrity

We believe that no one person has the monopoly of knowledge and therefore we shall value other people’s diverse views and cultures as well as an every-day execution of duties, we shall endeavor to uphold strong moral practices.

Effective E-learning Experience

We are committed to meeting the expectations of our clients and stakeholders to their satisfaction.
